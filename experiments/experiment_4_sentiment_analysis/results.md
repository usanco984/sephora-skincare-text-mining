# Experiment 4: Sentiment Analysis

## Goal

The goal of this experiment was to analyze the overall sentiment expressed in Sephora skincare product reviews.

Sentiment analysis was used to evaluate whether customer feedback was generally positive or negative.

---

## Method

RapidMiner's **Extract Sentiment** operator was applied to the review text.

The sentiment score measures the positive and negative tone expressed in the reviews.

---

## Sentiment Results

Common positive words observed:

- love
- great
- favorite
- beauty

Common negative words observed:

- hate
- terrible
- disappointed
- hurt

---

## Key Findings

The dataset overall shows **strong positive sentiment**, which aligns with the rating distribution.

Most reviews express satisfaction with the product experience.

---

## Additional Observation

When stemming was applied during preprocessing, the average sentiment score decreased slightly.

This suggests that preprocessing decisions can influence sentiment analysis results.

---

## Interpretation

Overall sentiment patterns suggest that Sephora skincare products receive generally positive customer feedback.

However, identifying negative terms can still provide useful signals for product improvement and customer experience analysis.
